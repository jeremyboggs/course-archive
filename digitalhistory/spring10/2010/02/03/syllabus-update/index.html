Syllabus Update

Because many of you have questions about modifying WordPress, I’ve decided to change next week’s practicum to “Modifying WordPress.” We’ll plan to cover Omeka on February 10. This will actually give you some time to try out the online version of Omeka beforehand, possibly even install it yourselves on your own server space! We’ll talk more about that tonight.

Additionally, we’ll have a guest speaker for next week’s talk. Tom Scheinfeldt, the Managing Director at the Center for History and New Media, and Co-Director of the Omeka project, will be joining us to talk about the impact of digital media on archives and museums.
