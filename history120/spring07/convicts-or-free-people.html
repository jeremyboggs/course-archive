Convicts or Free People

After looking at different advertisement for runaway slaves I learned something different about slaves. In each article the slaves were called English servant man or woman rather than slaves or Negro’s and the owners knew them really well physically and their personality. Another point the advertisements make is that the slaves use different methods to hide themselves so they can’t be found for example in the article on October 24th 1745 James Gibson who ran away lied to everyone who he came across and as noted by the owner as a cunning fellow. Another example of this was printed on November 11th 1747 where Margaret Barnes who ran away and claims to everyone that she could predict fortunes for others. The articles that I read did not state what skills the servants possessed and the reason behind that is that the owners did not what to alert others with the skills their servant’s possessed. This might be a problem in the process of getting them back since the servants will do what they do best in to earn a living while away from their masters.
In the last article that I read was the one where John Owl ran away from his master and the interesting part on the advertisement was that he ran away last on February 14th. This tells a lot about Mr. Owl who ran away more than once but was found and returned in the previous incidents. All the articles were very through with the different clothing that they wore right before fleeing off and also different kinds of scars or any other physical appearance that would identify them. The advertisements did a lot of great thing describing the servants.
Slaves are all kept against their will as said in the wikipedia article of slavery “Slaves are people who are held against their will since either their capture, their purchase, or their birth, and are deprived of their individual freedom for the purpose of exploitation of their labour and in some cases their sexual abuse.” [1] This tells us that they never had it easy with their masters so in return they ran away and never returned and if found they would try to find another way to escape such as Mr. Owl who ran away a couple of times.
All these advertisments shows us how much the owners miss their servants because of the output they gave to the owners such as farming, shoe maker and many more other things. Inaddition the advertisments also pointed out that they were not called slaves but instead called servant man or woman and the amount of information that they owners knew about them.

[1] http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Slavery
