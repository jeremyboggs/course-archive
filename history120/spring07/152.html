Women now and in the 50’s

Women have always been characterized and looked at differently by society as time has passed on through the years. From the way women are looked at in the kitchen and household, how women are looked at by men and their relationships with them, and to how women think about things they buy like an automobile. Over the time from the 50’s to present day today, some things have not changed, but a lot of the ways we viewed women today have changed since then.

The movie that I looked at from youtube is the Cooking Terms video. I thought that this movie degraded women and made them look like they had no intelligence and could not figure out the basic thing associated with cooking. In the beginning of the movie it shows Margie not being able to figure out the basics of creaming the butter and putting cream into the batter instead of creaming the butter and it messed up her cake. In todays society women are not looked at like that. However, most of the time women are the ones associated with cooking, but in the society today, they are looked at as intelligent individuals and not just a woman who can not figure out the basic things in a cookbook. We do not think of them not being able to follow directions. Another thing is it shows Margie’s husband going off to work and her staying home with nothing to do, but to cook for her newlywed husband of hers. This is really typical of life in the 50’s and what was expected of women, to stay home and take care of the house and cook for her husband. Today even though some women are housewives there are also plenty of working woman that do not stay home and have educated jobs. Back in the 50’s that was very rare to find, a woman working and not staying home. So that is something that has changed. Even though most of the woman that work do come home and make dinner, it is not unusual for a guy to make dinner like it was back then. The other thing that I noticed was how Margie served her husband, like she was his servant. She cut the cake then served him first and then herself. Today women are not expected to serve their husband. Men are expected to be able to get their own food for themselves and not be waited on.
Today the way women are viewed has changed significantly since the 50’s. In this clip I found of 30 minute meals(which is very bad recording and very bad clip by someone with nothing to do) it is proving how things have changed over time. Yes, it is still portraying women in the kitchen, but the show is to show how to make a dinner in 30 minutes or less. This is showing that women are not staying home and that they are out working and do not always have the time to make dinner and this is an easy way to do it. In the video from the 50’s all the things that were made were time consuming because all the women in the 50’s were portrayed to have all the time during the day. This is not the cause today because women are working and are not expected to stay at home and cook and clean.

There is another show from today, Food 911 on the food network and its hosted by Tyler Florence. I could not find a clip to show of it, but it is a how to show on cooking. It shows how to make certain foods that people have trouble with. This proves how times has changed because it is hosted by a guy. Back in the 50’s it would be pretty rare to see a cooking show hosted by a guy, or being portrayed doing a job that back then was thought of as a job that was suppose to be done by a woman. Its proving that women are not looked at as they need to be the ones in the kitchen making dinner in todays societ, that guys are capable of being in a kitchen too. I think it is putting women on an equal level with men, like they should be.

Even though women are still looked at today by some people not equal to men, it is no where close to how it was in the 50’s. Things have changed over that time span and women are looked at as very intelligent individuals today, not so much then. So even though some people think that women have not made a big movement and are still looked at as lesser individuals by some, it is by far no where close to how they were looked at and thought of back then.
