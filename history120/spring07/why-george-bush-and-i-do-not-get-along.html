Why George Bush and I Do Not Get Along

So i was reading this article and became very upset and thought I would blog about it.  Now Bush is saying that Iran is supplying all of these weapons to Iraq. I have been calling this for along time. Ever since Bush decieded to talk about the “Axis of Evil” (which consists of Iraq, Iran, and North Korea), I have been awaiting the day he brings the next country into the war plans. He has been looking for different ways to anger the people into wanting to attack Iran and now he finally came up with one. Who cares that it is not proven that Iran’s government is the one providing these weapons?”I’m going to do something about it,” Bush pledged, displaying apparent irritation at being repeatedly asked about mixed administration signals on who was behind the weaponry.

This quote angers me the most. Who cares if the American people want to get involved? No its up to Bush, and Bush alone to decide that. I can only say that I am thankful that the executive branch and the legislative branch are now majority two different political parties. I am also thankful that he is nearing the end of his regime, it is time to get a sane person back in the White House before our foreign relations are damaged beyond repair.
