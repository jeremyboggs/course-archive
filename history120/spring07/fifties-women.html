Fifties Women

In the past five decades society’s perception of women has changed. In the 1950’s, women were homemakers and men we the heads of the home. However, in the average American home does not consists of these two elements. Today, the perception of women is much different than the times our parents. Over the years women have become equal to men in the home and in the workplace.

The 1950’s were a different world than what it is today. During WWII, lives changed for everyday Americans. Women could not stay at home and be housewives any longer, they had to go out into the workforce while the men were off at war. For years, the women were heads of households, but that all changed once the war ended. When the 1950’s came around, society tried to put life back in order the way it was before. The men went off to work each morning and the women were sent back to the homes where they belonged. It was in this time that the Feminist movement began. At this time in history, and decades before, women were housewives, who came second to men. Women were forced to act, dress, and present themselves in specific ways without any argument. In the cooking clip, it shows how a woman, back in the 50’s, got married and then was tied down to a life of housework and pleasing her husband. “Cooking Terms!” This clip taught woman how to cook in order to serve her husband.

As we all know, women are not perceived in this light any longer. Women are no longer expected to stay at home while the men go to work. Women are even considered equal to men in the workforce, receiving equal jobs and wages. Women today have come a very long way from where they were just five decades before.

The Stepford Wives is a movie about a group of perfect families residing in a small town in present-day Connecticut. They were the perfect families because the husbands went to work each morning while the wives stayed home and mended the house. The women were perfect. They were blondes, they always looked presentable, and they were always second to their husbands. However, nothing is as it seems, the actual wives were made into robots by their husbands controlled with a remote. The clip I chose is with Bette Midler’s character as a robot and all of the abilities that make her a perfect wife. The Stepford Wives, As you can see, not only is her character gorgeous, she is the perfect wife. She cooks, cleans, vacuums, and mows all without ever needing to buy and appliance. She is even equipped with being able to offer a beer after a night together. She is the perfect wife.

Today, I think that society tries to push more of a balanced lifestyle. No longer do women need to stay at home or either work full time. Today, it is perfectly acceptable and very common to be a working mom. There is no question that the perception of women has changed in the past 5 decades. There will always be a select few that feel women are lesser than men, however, as a whole, I think the our society has finally found an equal way of life for us to live.
