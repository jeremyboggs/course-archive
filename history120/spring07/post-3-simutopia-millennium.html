Post # 3 Simutopia: Millennium

In my community , the resident ranges from newborns to the elderly. Anyone is welcome as long as they can accept the rules. Who isn’t allowed in our community are trouble makers and anyone who cannot accept our rules. Our community as a whole is very trusting and caring. Since the population is only 3,000, everyone knows how everyone is and during special events, we all get together to play games and socialize. The layout of my community is very simple and spacious. The brick houses where the residents live are usually grouped together so no one is living by their self alone unless you are the mayor (which is elected by the people of my community) of the community which needs to be separated from everyone else so they can oversee and protect the whole community. Near the homes of the residents are farmlands so they can plant their own vegetables and fruits so they can eat and sell it to the market. Near the farmland are the orchards for clean air and fresh fruits and nuts. The public building and the park is walking distance so you don’t have to drive to get there even there are roads to get there. On the other side of the river are the woods and the factory mill. You can tell that no one lives there. What is great about this community is that everyone is very hard working therefore, no one is homeless. Everyone is well to do. There will always be problems within a community such as drinking, sexual behaviors, and complex marriages therefore, we will have to work together to prevent this from happening.

In order to have a safe and trusting community, we must work together. From my point of view, the residents in my community are always working together when there’s a serious problem going on. Problems such as drinking, sexual behaviors, and complex marriages which are mention on this link. These problems that are happening in my community is very devastating because not is it only hurting us individually, but it is hurting us as a whole community.

The goal for my community is to solve our most serious problems which are drinking, sexual behaviors, and complex marriages. The reason for solving these serious issues is so that future generations won’t be exposed to these serious issues. These serious issues are damaging and will tear this wonderful community apart. Without the exposure to these serious issues, everyone in the community will be safer and there will not be a lot of kids in the orphanages. In conclusion, these problems and issues need to be solved because it will tear up this community
